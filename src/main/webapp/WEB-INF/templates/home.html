Welcome

This web site provides functionality to help the Eagle's Wings Investment Club (EWIC) develop and refine criteria for disciplined selling of positions, and to monitor positions using those criteria with as little effort as possible. Club experience has demonstrated that a disciplined, fundamentals-based approach to stock selection and purchase consistently provides a stream of good portfolio options. Club experience has also shown that trying to make timely decisions around the sale of deteriorating portfolio positions rarely can be done in the context of analyzing changes in underlying company fundamentals at monthly club meetings.

Selling is a much more emotional process than buying, and it often requires more responsive decision making than monthly club meetings provide for. The combination of these two realities has resulted in punishing losses for the EWIC portfolio during periods of significant market and economic downturn. Occasional losses are a fact-of-life for investors. Limiting those losses to manageable, minor amounts is an important distinction between skilled and novice investors. Hopefully this tool will help our club to avoid large losses in the future.
